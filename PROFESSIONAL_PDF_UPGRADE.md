### Technical Implementation

**File**: `frontend/src/lib/pdfGenerator.ts`

**Color Palette**:
- Primary Blue: `[41, 98, 255]` - Headers, titles
- Light Blue: `[240, 245, 255]` - Background boxes
- Dark Gray: `[51, 51, 51]` - Main text
- Light Gray: `[128, 128, 128]` - Secondary text

**Typography**:
- Company Name: 18pt Bold
- INVOICE Title: 32pt Bold Blue
- Section Headers: 9-10pt Bold
- Body Text: 9pt Normal
- Table Headers: 10pt Bold White on Blue

**Layout Structure**:
1. Header (Company + Invoice Title)
2. Details Blocks (2 columns with backgrounds)
3. Items Table (Full width, professional grid)
4. Summary (Right-aligned, highlighted)
5. Terms (Full width, small text)
6. Footer (Centered, gray)
